Our Mission

As supporters of order in the galaxy, we will be tasked with securing and maintaining peace from those who oppose us. Our instructions will change based on what Lord Vader needs, but will be necessary to bring forth the emperor's vision.

Procedure to Enter

You as soldiers will enter a serious a test that will test strength, agility, mental state, and loyalty to the empire. These test will show what command you will follow, possibly being under Lord Vader or even the emperor himself. Good Luck Soldier!

If FAILURE

Failing is inexcusable! If you fail through the trails and you are not placed among the ranks, you will be dropped from the program. Reentering the next year is acceptable if Lord Vader allows it. Just know next time be extra prepared. Lord Vader will want to see growth mentally.
